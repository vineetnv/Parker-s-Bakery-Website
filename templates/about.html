About SweetBite Bakery

Welcome to Astral Bakery! We bake fresh, delicious cookies daily using only the finest ingredients. Our mission is to bring joy to your taste buds with every bite. From classic chocolate chip to nutty peanut butter and spiced oatmeal raisin, we have something for every cookie lover.

We are family-owned and dedicated to creating a warm, friendly experience for all our customers. Whether you're picking up for a special occasion or just craving a sweet treat, we take pride in making your day a little sweeter.
